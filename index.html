About DJ Rayyiz

DJ Rayyiz is a visionary artist blending electronic beats with soulful melodies. With years of experience in the industry, he captivates audiences worldwide with his unique style and infectious energy. Influenced by the pioneers of electronic music, Rayyiz pushes boundaries to create unforgettable experiences.

From intimate club sets to massive festivals, his sets are a journey through soundscapes that resonate with the heart and move the soul.
